Modulor · Massing
Modulor Massing generates parametric building compositions using Le Corbusier's Modulor Blue Series — a Fibonacci-scaled sequence based on the golden ratio and the human figure. Every dimension (bay widths, floor heights, relief depths) is drawn from this series, so proportions are always harmonically related.
